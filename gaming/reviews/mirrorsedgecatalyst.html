mirror's edge catalyst

EA DICE PC 2016

bad story - I honestly didn't even realize this was a prequel until significantly into the game. I was just confused.
great gameplay - this game improved on its unique but unpolished predecessor! touches like quick swings around corners and a grappling hook seemed like low-use gimmicks at first, but ended up adding a lot to smooth out the parkour.
diverse cast - two black women, two white men, one asian man and our main asian lady. zero objectification of any of them. fantastic.


this is the second underrated game in an underrated series!
